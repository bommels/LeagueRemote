Getting started

Step 1. Download League Remote PC

League Remote PC is an application that runs on your PC and reads from the League of Legends client.
Windows: v0.1 | Mac: Coming soon

Step 2. Run League Remote PC

Place the league_remote.exe in your League of Legends program folder. The default location is in C:\Riot Games\League of Legends. Make sure it is the same folder as the LeagueClient application. After that, double click on the application to start. Windows can ask for network permission, you need to accept this to run the application. If the application is running successfully, you will see the following: Take a note of the IP address shown after League Remote Location. You will need this in the next step. You can minimze this window.

Step 3. Download League Remote App

Download the League Remote App from the Play Store or App Store.
Android: League Remote | iOS: Coming soon

After installing, click the settings icon and fill in the IP address displayed on League Remote PC (step 2) and click save. Make sure your phone is on the same network as your PC! After a couple of seconds your app will be connected to your client! You can now start your League of Legends client and log in.
